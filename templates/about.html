What is TARS?

The Automation And Robotics Society 2.0 of IIIT-Bh brings you to the world of technology .We provide the best experience with Robotics,Artificial Intelligence , Machine Learning, Automation and much more. Whether you like to create, invent or innovate or you would like to appease your curiosity about the various marvels of technology, TARS 2.0 is just the place. Inspiring workshops, insightful sessions by tech experts,lots of fun activities and competitions, and a lot more await you here.So get ready to immerse yourself with mind-blowing wisdom and to get in touch with tomorrow.

We simply simplify tech.
